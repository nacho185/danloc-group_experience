The 4 Main Steps

Saw Machine

Cut Studs To Length

Start by getting a long bar that’s about 12 feet. Then, use a saw to cut it to the size you want.

Chamfer Machine

Cut Edge

Use a tool to carefully cut and bevel the edge, creating a smooth, angled finish.

Stamper

Gives It Material Type

A stamping machine is used to press patterns or designs onto metal bolts, creating precise impressions on their surface.

Theading Machine

Form The Threads

A threading machine is a tool that cuts grooves into metal rods or pipes to create threads, allowing nuts or fittings to be screwed onto them securely.
